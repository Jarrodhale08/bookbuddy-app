[ ] APP STORE PRODUCTS (iOS)
    1. Log into App Store Connect
    2. Go to Your App -> In-App Purchases
    3. Create auto-renewable subscription group "BookBuddy Premium"
    4. Create products:
       - bookbuddy_monthly: $4.99/month
       - bookbuddy_yearly: $39.99/year (33% savings)
       - bookbuddy_lifetime: $99.99 (optional)
    5. Configure 7-day free trial:
       - Edit subscription -> Introductory Offers
       - Add Free Trial: 7 days

[ ] GOOGLE PLAY PRODUCTS (Android)

    2. Go to Monetize -> Subscriptions
    3. Create matching products:
       - bookbuddy_monthly: $4.99/month
       - bookbuddy_yearly: $39.99/year
    4. Add free trial offer:
       - Edit subscription -> Offers -> Free trial: 7 days
